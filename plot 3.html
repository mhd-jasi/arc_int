Formula One Modern

This Modern house high on the slopes of the North Shore is inspired by
the organic lines of racing cars. The Owner these, and other race cars
around the world including the famous 24 hour LeMans race The home
sits on a 300 foot wide site with a 180 degree view of the Lower
Mainland. In addition to taking every advantage of the spectacular views,
this home features carb parking for up to 14 vehicles, many of which are
seen through glass from within the home. The Residence is organized
with all Living, Office and Bedroom functions on the top floor- with an
emphasis on indoor outdoor flow of space and connection to the Garden
Recreation, Entry Foyer and car storage are located on the Main level
along with outdoor pool and entertaining areas. The Basement
accommodates the theatre, gym, wine, storage and staff.
